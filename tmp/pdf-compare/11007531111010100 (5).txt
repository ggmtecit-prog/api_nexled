LLED Barra 24V 10 WW273 HE
The 24V linear bars Nexled are a versatile lighting solution that can be applied in
showcases, furniture, general or decorative lighting equipment.
Great flexibility of sizes, (10cm to 225cm) with 13W/m, various colour
temperatures and light output angles.
Easy to install, it allows the use of individual bars and/or joining several bars in a
continuous line up to 6 meters.
Colour temperature Warm White 2700K CRI >80
Colour temperature margin of 3 SDCM with reference to the centre of the
MacAdam ellipse according to ANSI C78.377:2011.
Energy class according to EU regulation 2019/2015.
